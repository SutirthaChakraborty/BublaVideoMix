Best Photo Studio Serving All of Jalpaiguri District

🗓️ March 2025 ⏱️ 5 min read 📍 Based in Maynaguri

Photo Square — Maynaguri's Studio for All of Jalpaiguri

Located at the heart of Maynaguri town, Photo Square is strategically positioned to serve the entire Jalpaiguri district of West Bengal. With excellent road connectivity via NH 27 and the Jalpaiguri-Siliguri highway, residents from Dhupguri, Mal Bazaar, Rajganj, Kranti, Matiali, Gorumara and surrounding areas regularly visit Photo Square for services they can't find locally.

For wedding videography, large-format printing, photo restoration and custom gift orders, Photo Square also provides on-site service and home delivery throughout the district — bringing professional photography services directly to your doorstep.

Areas Served by Photo Square

Maynaguri & Nearby Blocks

Our home base. Maynaguri is a thriving sub-divisional town in Jalpaiguri district with a growing population and active business community. Photo Square has served Maynaguri residents across all its wards and surrounding village panchayats for over 15 years.

Dhupguri

Dhupguri, located about 30 km north of Maynaguri, is one of the largest towns in Jalpaiguri district. Photo Square regularly serves Dhupguri families for wedding videography, passport photos and bulk photo printing. Our wedding team frequently travels to Dhupguri for full-day wedding coverage.

Mal Bazaar

Nestled in the foothills of the Dooars, Mal Bazaar is surrounded by tea gardens that provide stunning backdrops for pre-wedding films and portraits. Photo Square serves Mal Bazaar clients for photography sessions, photo restoration and custom gifts. The scenic tea garden settings of Mal Bazaar are particularly popular for pre-wedding shoots.

Rajganj

Rajganj, near Jalpaiguri town, is served by Photo Square for print services, passport photos and event videography. Clients from Rajganj find Photo Square's location in Maynaguri easily accessible via bus from Jalpaiguri.

Kranti & Matiali

The densely forested Kranti and Matiali blocks border the Gorumara National Park — making them unique settings for nature and portrait photography. Photo Square's team has covered weddings and cultural events in this area, and local residents travel to Maynaguri for printing and restoration services.

Gorumara & Dooars Region

The Dooars is one of the most beautiful regions in West Bengal — home to tea gardens, elephant corridors and dense forests. Photo Square offers wildlife photography guidance, travel photo printing and Dooars-location pre-wedding shoots for couples who want a truly unique backdrop for their story.

📦 Delivery & Outstation Service: Bulk print orders and custom gifts can be shipped to any address in Jalpaiguri district. Contact us on WhatsApp for courier delivery options and timelines.

Why Jalpaiguri District Residents Choose Photo Square

Quality Not Available Locally

Most villages and smaller towns in Jalpaiguri district don't have access to professional-grade photo studios. Photo Square fills this gap — offering Siliguri and Kolkata-level quality without the travel, cost and hassle of going to a bigger city.

WhatsApp-Based Remote Ordering

Residents of Dhupguri, Mal Bazaar and other distant areas can send photos via WhatsApp, place their order remotely, and either collect from the studio or arrange delivery. For restoration jobs, a clear WhatsApp photo of the damaged original is enough to begin the process.

Experienced with Local Customs & Traditions

The team at Photo Square deeply understands the rituals, ceremonies and cultural traditions of Jalpaiguri district — including Bengali Hindu weddings, tea garden community celebrations, Rajbanshi and Rabha cultural events. This local knowledge ensures that our photography and videography captures the true essence of each occasion.
