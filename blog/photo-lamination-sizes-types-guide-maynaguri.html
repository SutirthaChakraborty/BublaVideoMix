✨ Types of Photo Lamination

1. Gloss Lamination (Shiny Finish)

Gloss lamination gives your photo a shiny, high-reflective surface. Colours appear brighter, more vibrant and saturated. It is the classic lamination look that most people recognise from studio photographs.

Best for: Wedding photos, birthday photos, portraits, colourful event photos, gifts

Available sizes: All sizes from 4×6 to 24×36 at Photo Square

2. Matte Lamination (Non-Reflective Finish)

Matte lamination gives a soft, silky, non-shiny surface. It eliminates fingerprints, reduces glare under lights and gives a sophisticated, professional look. Many professional photographers prefer matte for portrait work.

Best for: Certificates, professional portraits, office display photos, photos displayed under bright lighting

3. Waterproof Lamination

Waterproof lamination seals the photo completely from moisture. Essential in Jalpaiguri's humid climate and monsoon season. Waterproof laminated photos can be displayed in bathrooms, kitchens, shop fronts and outdoor areas.

Available in: Both matte and gloss finishes

4. Self-Adhesive Lamination

Self-adhesive lamination has a pressure-sensitive adhesive backing — allowing the laminated photo to be stuck directly onto a surface (wall, album, board) without additional glue. Popular for DIY photo projects and custom décor.
