Top Birthday Gift Ideas from Photo Square

1. Personalised Photo Mug — ₹199 onwards

A classic that never goes out of style. Custom ceramic mugs printed with your favourite photo and a personal birthday message make a warm, everyday reminder of your love. Choose from standard white mugs, magic mugs (reveal on heating), and coloured inner mugs. Printed with dye-sublimation for vibrant, lasting colour.

2. Laminated Photo Frame — ₹250 onwards

A high-quality printed and laminated photo, mounted in a premium wooden or metal frame, makes one of the most personal birthday gifts possible. Whether it's a childhood photo, a couple's portrait or a family group shot, a professionally framed print instantly becomes a cherished keepsake. Available in gloss or matte lamination, in sizes from 4×6 to 24×36 inches.

3. Canvas Photo Print — ₹699 onwards

Transform a favourite birthday photo into a stunning canvas wall art piece. Gallery-wrap canvas prints look impressive in any living room, bedroom or office — and make the birthday person feel truly special. Available in sizes from 8×10 up to 24×36 inches.

4. Photo Cushion — ₹449 onwards

A soft, custom-printed cushion featuring a beloved photo — ideal for room décor gifts. Available in 12×12 and 16×16 inch sizes with a polyester cover and soft fill. Birthday recipients love seeing their face (or their pet's face!) on a cosy cushion.

5. Photo Keychain — ₹99 onwards

A pocket-sized memory that travels everywhere with the birthday person. Metal or acrylic keychains with a photo insert are popular as school farewell gifts, friendship gifts and small token presents for relatives. Affordable enough for group gifting.

6. Personalised Photo Calendar — ₹499 onwards

Give the gift of a full year of memories. A 12-month wall calendar featuring the birthday person's own photos — family moments, travel snapshots, celebrations — is a truly unique and practical gift. Available in A3 and A2 sizes.

🎂 Birthday Tip: Combine a photo mug, a framed print and a keychain into a birthday hamper for an impressive gift set that's still more affordable than most store-bought luxury gifts in Jalpaiguri!
