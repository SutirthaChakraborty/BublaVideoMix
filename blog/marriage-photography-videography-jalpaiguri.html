Our Marriage Photography Services

Candid & Traditional Photography

We offer both traditional posed photography (formal family portraits, ceremony shots) and candid documentary photography (spontaneous emotional moments captured naturally). Many families in Maynaguri and Jalpaiguri district now prefer a mix of both styles — classic portraits for albums and candid shots for storytelling.

HD & 4K Videography

Your marriage film is something you will watch repeatedly for years. Our 4K videography team uses cinema-grade cameras, professional stabilisers and wireless audio to capture your marriage with stunning clarity. We offer:

  • Full-day documentary coverage (multi-camera)
  • Cinematic highlight film (3–7 minutes, set to music)
  • Drone aerial shots of the venue and procession
  • Same-day edit (highlights shared on your wedding night)
  • Full edited film delivered within 30 days on USB and cloud link

Marriage Album Design

The wedding album is the final, tangible product of your marriage photography — a family heirloom that will be passed down through generations. Photo Square designs and prints premium wedding albums in-house:

Passport & Document Photos for Marriage Registrations

Planning to register your marriage under the Special Marriage Act or Hindu Marriage Act? Photo Square provides instant government-compliant passport-size and stamp-size photos for both parties — accepted at all Jalpaiguri district courts and marriage registration offices, with the correct white background, matt finish and official dimensions.
