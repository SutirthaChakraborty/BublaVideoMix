Your Old Photos Deserve a Second Life

In every family's drawer or album, there are old photographs — faded with age, yellowed by time, creased from years of handling, or damaged by floods, moisture and insects. These are not just pictures: they are the faces of grandparents who are no longer with us, wedding moments from decades past, and childhood photographs that no digital backup can replace.

Photo Square in Maynaguri specialises in professionally restoring these irreplaceable images. Using a combination of AI-powered restoration tools and expert manual digital editing, we bring old photographs back to life — and in many cases, make them look better than the originals ever did.

What Types of Old Photos Can We Restore?

Our restoration service at Maynaguri covers virtually any type of photographic damage:

  • 🟡 Faded & yellowed photos — colour and contrast correction to restore original tones
  • 😢 Torn & missing sections — digital reconstruction of missing areas, faces and backgrounds
  • 💧 Water & flood damaged — stain removal, mould spotting, paper damage correction
  • 🐛 Insect-eaten photos — restoration of areas eaten by silverfish and other insects
  • 📦 Crumpled & creased prints — digital flattening and crease removal
  • 🔲 Low-resolution old scans — AI upscaling and sharpening for high-detail enlargements
  • ⬛ Overexposed or underexposed photos — tonal correction and detail recovery
  • 🖊️ Scratched & scuffed prints — removal of surface scratches and blemishes

Black & White Photo Colorization

One of the most emotionally powerful services Photo Square offers is bringing colour to black-and-white photographs. Seeing your grandparents' wedding photo in full, natural colour for the first time, or watching a 1960s family portrait come to life with the hues of saris, flowers and skin tones — it is an experience that moves people deeply.

We use AI-assisted colorization combined with manual refinement to ensure the colours look natural, not artificial. Our editors study context clues in the photo — fabric textures, outdoor vegetation, skin tones — to produce results that feel authentic to the era.

🎁 Unique Gift Idea: A colorized and restored version of a grandparent's old photo, professionally printed and framed, makes one of the most emotionally meaningful birthday or anniversary gifts you can give.

AI-Enhanced Photo Restoration

Photo Square uses the latest AI restoration technology — the same tools used by professional archivists worldwide — to handle photo repair tasks that would previously have taken many hours of manual work. AI helps us:

  • Enhance facial details — sharpen blurred or unclear faces in old portraits
  • Upscale resolution — turn a small, pixelated scan into a large, detailed print
  • Remove noise and grain — clean up old film grain while preserving detail
  • Reconstruct missing portions — intelligently fill in torn corners and missing sections
  • Remove backgrounds — isolate subjects from damaged backgrounds

After AI processing, our human editors review and refine every result — we never deliver a purely automated output without professional oversight.
