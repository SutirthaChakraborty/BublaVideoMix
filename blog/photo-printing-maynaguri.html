Print Sizes & Options Available

Photo Square offers a comprehensive range of print formats to suit every requirement:

Standard Print Sizes

  • Wallet / ID size — 1.5×2 inches, perfect for ID cards and documents
  • 4×6 inches — the classic photo print, ideal for albums and frames
  • 5×7 inches — great for desk frames and greeting cards
  • 8×10 inches — popular for wall hanging and gifts
  • 8×12 inches — full panoramic desktop size
  • 12×18 inches — large wall prints

Specialty Printing

  • Canvas prints — stretched canvas for a gallery-quality look
  • Matte prints — non-glare finish, ideal for portraits
  • Gloss prints — high-shine finish for vivid colour photography
  • Satin/pearl finish — between gloss and matte, excellent for wedding photos
  • Panoramic prints — wide landscape and wedding venue shots
  • Photo books and albums — professionally bound, lay-flat designs
💡 Tip: For best print quality, provide photos with a minimum resolution of 300 DPI at the desired print size. Our team can advise you on the optimal size for your image quality.
