Photo Lamination Services

Lamination protects your printed photos from moisture, dust, fingerprints and physical damage — significantly extending their lifespan. Photo Square offers professional cold and hot lamination in multiple finishes:

  • Gloss lamination — high shine, vivid colours; ideal for colourful prints and event photos
  • Matte lamination — non-reflective, anti-glare; excellent for portraits and document photos
  • Soft-touch (velvet) lamination — premium silky feel; popular for framed gifts and certificates
  • Holographic lamination — decorative rainbow shimmer; great for birthday and festive prints

Lamination available from A6 (postcard size) to A0 (large format). Same-day lamination for standard sizes.
