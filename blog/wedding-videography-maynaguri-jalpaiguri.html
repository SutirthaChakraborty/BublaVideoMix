Capturing Your Wedding Story in Cinematic 4K

Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life — and the memories deserve to be preserved in the highest quality possible. Photo Square's wedding videography team has covered hundreds of weddings across Maynaguri, Dhupguri, Mal Bazaar, Rajganj and throughout Jalpaiguri district, crafting cinematic films that couples treasure for a lifetime.

From intimate family ceremonies to grand wedding receptions, our experienced videographers capture every emotional moment — the first look, the exchange of garlands, the tearful farewells and the joyful celebrations — with professional artistry and care.

Our Wedding Videography Packages

Highlight Film (Short Film)

A 3–5 minute cinematic highlight reel set to music, capturing the best moments of your wedding day. Perfect for sharing on social media and with family members who couldn't attend. Delivered in Full HD (1080p) or 4K.

Full Wedding Film

A comprehensive documentary-style recording of your complete wedding — covering the Aashirwad, Holud/Gaye Holud, Akal Bodhan, wedding ceremony, reception and all related events. Full HD multi-camera coverage ensures no moment is missed.

Pre-Wedding Film

A beautiful pre-wedding cinematic video at scenic locations around Jalpaiguri district — tea gardens, river banks, forest paths or urban settings. These are cherished keepsakes and make wonderful additions to wedding invitations.

Drone Aerial Coverage

Add a breathtaking dimension to your wedding film with licensed drone aerial videography. Wide-angle shots of the venue, processions and outdoor ceremonies create a truly cinematic feel that sets your wedding video apart.

📅 Book Early: Peak wedding season in Jalpaiguri (November–February and Auspicious dates during summer) fills up fast. We recommend booking at least 3–4 months in advance to secure your preferred team and date.

Equipment We Use

Photo Square's wedding team uses professional cinema-grade equipment to ensure your film looks and sounds exceptional:

  • 4K digital cinema cameras for stunningly detailed footage
  • Professional stabilisers and gimbals for smooth, cinematic movement
  • Wireless lapel microphones for crystal-clear audio of vows and speeches
  • Professional lighting rigs for evening and indoor ceremonies
  • DJI drones for aerial perspectives
  • Multi-camera setup to capture every angle simultaneously

Post-Production & Editing

The magic truly happens in the editing suite. Our skilled editors at Photo Square use professional software to:

  • Colour grade footage to a warm, cinematic look
  • Synchronise audio from multiple sources
  • Add tasteful music, transitions and titles
  • Create emotional storytelling arcs that honour your unique love story
  • Deliver the final film in multiple formats for TV, phone and social media
