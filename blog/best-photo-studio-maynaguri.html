Unlike smaller studios that specialise in just one or two services, Photo Square is a full-service photography centre covering every photographic need:

  • Digital photo printing (4×6, 5×7, 8×10, large format, canvas)

  • Wedding photography & HD videography
  • Photo restoration and digital repair
  • Photo lamination (matte and gloss)
  • Custom photo gifts: mugs, keychains, calendars, cushions
  • Event and family portrait photography
  • Photo framing with premium frames
  • HD video editing and event coverage

3. State-of-the-Art Equipment

Photo Square uses professional-grade printers, digital cameras and editing workstations to ensure that every print is sharp, colour-accurate and long-lasting. The studio regularly updates its equipment to keep pace with the latest photographic technology — giving Maynaguri residents access to big-city quality without travelling to Siliguri or Kolkata.

4. Experienced Team with Local Knowledge

The team at Photo Square understands local customs, traditions and the unique settings of Jalpaiguri district — from lush tea garden backdrops to the vibrant Durga Puja festivities. This local expertise translates into photography and videography that genuinely captures the spirit of each occasion.
