Photo Types We Provide

Indian Passport Photos

Standard 35mm × 45mm colour photos with plain white background, printed on matte finish paper. Fully compliant with Government of India Passport Seva Kendra specifications — correct head size ratio, no shadows, eyes open and facing forward.

Visa Photos (All Countries)

We produce visa photos for all major embassies and consulates, including:

  • US Visa — 2×2 inch (50×50mm), white background
  • UK Visa & BRP — 35×45mm, cream/off-white background
  • Schengen Visa (Europe) — 35×45mm, light grey/white
  • Canada Visa — 50×70mm, white background
  • Australia Visa — 35×45mm, white background
  • UAE/Gulf Visa — 40×60mm, white background

Aadhaar & ID Card Photos

Aadhaar enrolment and update photos with the correct specifications. We also provide ID card photos for:

  • PAN Card applications
  • Voter ID (EPIC) photos
  • Driving Licence photos
  • Bank account opening
  • School and college ID cards
  • Government scheme enrollments (PMJAY, PM Awas, etc.)
✅ Guaranteed Acceptance: If your passport photo from Photo Square is rejected by any government office (which almost never happens), we will retake and reprint your photos free of charge.

What Makes Our Passport Photos Different?

Many phone-based or instant kiosk passport photos are rejected because of incorrect specifications. At Photo Square, a professional photographer takes your photo with:

  • Professional studio lighting — no shadows on face or background
  • Calibrated white seamless background — meets all government standards
  • High-resolution digital capture — sharp, detailed prints
  • Expert retouching — minor blemishes removed without altering appearance
  • Correct cropping — precise head-to-frame ratio per document specifications
  • Digital copy provided — e-mail or WhatsApp the digital file for online applications

Also Available: Digital Passport Photo Files

For online passport and visa applications, we also provide a digital passport photo file (JPEG, correct size and DPI) that you can upload directly to the Passport Seva Online Portal, NRI Services or embassy websites. No more rejected online uploads.

Bulk Passport Photos for Institutions
